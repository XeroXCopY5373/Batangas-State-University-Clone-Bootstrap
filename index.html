Overview of Batangas State University ARASOF Nasugbu Campus

The Batangas State University ARASOF Nasugbu campus, situated at coordinates 14.0670°N and 120.6261°E, is bordered by Nasugbu Poblacion to the North and East, Brgy. Bucana to the South, and Nasugbu Bay to the West. Notable landmarks nearby include the Globe Telecom Building and Kainan sa Dalampasigan Restaurant. The campus is easily accessible via public transport services from Manila, including routes from PITX, Coastal Road, and Pasay bus terminals. It can also be reached by car, with two main routes: one through Tagaytay City and Tagaytay-Nasugbu Highway and another via Cavitex Highway and the Nasugbu-Ternate Highway, passing through a subterranean tunnel in Mount Palay Palay. The campus covers an area of 4.159 hectares, divided into two sections. Area I, near the Nasugbu shore, houses most of the campus buildings, including the administration building, while the second section is known as the Physical Education School Sports (PESS) Ground or Roxas-Gargollo Sports Field, covering 1.6849 hectares. This spacious field is used for physical education, military training, sports events, and various outdoor activities, and it also features the Batstate U Hostel and the Higher Education Building, where many classes are held.
